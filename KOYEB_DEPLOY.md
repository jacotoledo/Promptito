# Free Hosting with Koyeb

## Deploy in 3 Steps

1. **Fork this repo** on GitHub
2. **Go to** [app.koyeb.com](https://app.koyeb.com)
3. **Sign up** → **Create Service** → **Deploy from GitHub** → select your fork → **Deploy**

Your app is live at `https://your-app.koyeb.app`

## Keep It Awake

Koyeb sleeps after 30 days. Add a free [UptimeRobot](https://uptimerobot.com) monitor to keep it running.

## Uninstall

Delete the service in your Koyeb dashboard. Done.
